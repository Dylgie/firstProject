Description

The Deere 212 was the from the economy line of garden tractors from the earl 1970's ubtil mid 1980's. It came with two options for the lift system, hydraulic and manual. You could also purchase an electric lift kit to install on the tractors. The 212 could run a mid-mount mower deck, as well as a rotary tiller, front blade, and snow thrower.
